From Hurdles to Hustle

Every startup faces obstacles. Here’s a look at the key challenges we encountered and the strategies we implemented to overcome them.

Challenge: Time Management

As full-time students, balancing academic responsibilities with business activities was demanding, affecting our productivity.

Solution: Structured Division of Roles

We divided responsibilities based on strengths. This parallel workflow balanced our workload and made us more efficient.

Challenge: High Market Competition

The campus snack market is crowded. It was difficult to attract attention without a large advertising budget.

Solution: Grassroots Marketing

We used word-of-mouth and social media sharing. Our relatable online presence helped us build real connections and grow organically.

Challenge: Limited Startup Capital

Our initial RM150 funding limited our ability to invest in professional packaging or bulk ingredient purchases.

Solution: Starting Small, Scaling Smart

We kept our first batch small to test demand. We then reinvested profits, allowing us to grow steadily without financial risk.
